Select Consignment Slots Have Now Re-Opened

Consignment of Graded and Ungraded Card Collections are have reopened for select cards and collections. Please contact us regarding your consignment. Our consignment process is swift and easy. Pacific Memorabilia is fully insured, with a complete digital consignment process. We carefully intake your cards, photograph them, inventory, and take care of shipping and returns.

Sales reports for your consigned cards are emailed to you the moment your card sells.

All ungraded cards are carefully photographed, inventoried, and penny-sleeved and placed in (and shipped in) Industry Standard Card Savers (same as used for submission to PSA or Beckett). Some fees may apply. Contact us for more details. We can even arrange pickup in select locations, as well as provide direct "mail-back shipping" materials and postage which we mail to you, you package, and ship back to us for Consignment.

We Combine Shipping

We combine shipping on both Graded and Ungraded cards. This is based on the final sale amount of the combined purchase, the type of card (Graded or Ungraded). Please contact us before or directly after a purchase for the best combined shipping rates. All combined shipments under $1,000 are shipped via USPS. Alternative shipping arrangements can be made both before and after purchase.
